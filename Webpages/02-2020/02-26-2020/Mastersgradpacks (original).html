Congratulations Class of 2020!

Step 1: Register for Commencement 
March 4 - May 23, 2020

All Campus Commencement: Register at commencement.ucsd.edu by May 23 for your ticket to participate in the All Campus Commencement ceremony. All tickets ordered by Wednesday, May 23, will be available for printing on the registration website from Thursday, May 24, through Sunday, June 14, 2020.
 
Students must print their own ticket and bring it with them to the All Campus Commencement ceremony. The ticket can be reprinted in the event that it becomes lost or damaged. Note: The first scan of the ticket at each ceremony will be the only use, other attempted entries will be denied.
 
Guest tickets are not required for All Campus Commencement or the graduate ceremonies. Family and friends are welcome to attend.
 
Graduate Division Commencements: Register at commencement.ucsd.edu by May 23 to participate in the Graduate Division ceremonies. A physical ticket is not required for graduates or their guests.
 
Profession School Commencements: Contact your division or school for information about participating in your ceremony. Guest tickets are not required for the professional school ceremonies.
