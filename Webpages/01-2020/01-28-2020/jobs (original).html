JOB 1: STDT 3/COMPUTER ASSISTANT

 
Job Number: 3415163
Job Title: STDT 3/COMPUTER ASSISTANT
Wage/Salary: $14.00/hr
Work Schedule: Up to 19 hours/week (up to 30 hrs/wk during summer)
Location: UCSD Bookstore, La Jolla, CA
Number of Openings: 1
Start Date: ASAP

This is a temporary position running through winter quarter only, with an end date of Saturday March 21st, 2020.
There is a possibility that the position will be extended after that depending on departmental needs.



Job Description:

Data entry using catalogs, worksheets & sales orders. Assist computer store customers with computer related purchases. Print, sort & route reports. Receive packing list data and compare to purchase orders. Print receiving documents and tags. Assist computer buyers and staff with special projects as needed. Post completed paperwork and match reports. Assist in rotating, moving, and creating attractive merchandise displays. Assist with cashiering as needed.



Qualifications:

Demonstrated ability to work in a retail environment, including cashiering. Demonstrated knowledge of Macintosh and Windows computers, tablets, and peripherals; demonstrated interpersonal skills. Excellent data entry, word processing, typing & 10-key skills. Familiarity with Office software applications. Prior experience in retail, merchandising, and creating displays preferred.



Special Conditions of Employment

Work schedule: up to 19 hrs/week Monday-Saturday (up to 30 hrs/week during summer). Must be able to work Saturdays, occasional Sundays, evenings, and holidays. Background check required. Working weekends is required.
Work-study preferred but not required.
Must Pay UCSD student services fees each quarter working.



Core Competencies

  • Professionalism & Integrity
  • Oral Written & Digital Communication
  • Digital Information Fluency
