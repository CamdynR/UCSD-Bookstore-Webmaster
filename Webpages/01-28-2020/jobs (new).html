JOB 1: STDT 3/COMPUTER ASSISTANT

 
Job Number: 3415163
Job Title: STDT 3/COMPUTER ASSISTANT
Wage/Salary: $14.00/hr
Work Schedule: Up to 19 hours/week (up to 30 hrs/wk during summer)
Location: UCSD Bookstore, La Jolla, CA
Number of Openings: 1
Start Date: ASAP

This is a temporary position running through winter quarter only, with an end date of Saturday March 21st, 2020.

Job Description:

Data entry using catalogs, worksheets & sales orders. Assist computer store customers with computer related purchases. Print, sort & route reports. Receive packing list data and compare to purchase orders. Print receiving documents and tags. Assist computer buyers and staff with special projects as needed. Post completed paperwork and match reports. Assist in rotating, moving, and creating attractive merchandise displays. Assist with cashiering as needed.



Qualifications:

Demonstrated ability to work in a retail environment, including cashiering. Demonstrated knowledge of Macintosh and Windows computers, tablets, and peripherals; demonstrated interpersonal skills. Excellent data entry, word processing, typing & 10-key skills. Familiarity with Office software applications. Prior experience in retail, merchandising, and creating displays preferred.



Special Conditions of Employment

Work schedule: up to 19 hrs/week Monday-Saturday (up to 30 hrs/week during summer). Must be able to work Saturdays, occasional Sundays, evenings, and holidays. Background check required. Working weekends is required.
Work-study preferred but not required.
Must Pay UCSD student services fees each quarter working.



Core Competencies

  • Professionalism & Integrity
  • Oral Written & Digital Communication
  • Digital Information Fluency

JOB 3: CUSTOMER SERVICE TEMP ASSOCIATE/STDT2

 
Job Number: 3468710
Job Title: CUSTOMER SERVICE TEMP ASSOCIATE/STDT2
Wage/Salary: $13.00/hr
Work Schedule: Up to 19 hours/week
Location: UCSD Bookstore, La Jolla, CA
Number of Openings: 3
Start Date: Feb 3rd, 2020


Position Overview:

This is a temporary position running from Febrary 3 through March 21st. A limited number of permanent positions may be offered with an increase from $13.00 to $13.25 at the end of the temporary appointment based on job performance, schedule, and needs of the bookstore.
Associate shifts are between the hours of 7:45am-6:15pm Monday-Friday and 10:45am-4:15pm on Saturday. During finals week the Bookstore opens 30 minutes earlier. Must be able to work some Saturdays. You will be scheduled between 12-18 hours per week (the opportunity to work more hours when school is not in session will be offered). If you have another on campus job you cannot work more than 29 hours total between each job. Please consider this if you have multiple jobs on campus as all hours worked will count toward this total.



Job Description:

Cashiering

  1. This will be a cashiering position, ringing customers out at the register.
  2. Greet customers and help them find product.
  3. Help customers pick up online orders.
  4. Direct customers to open registers, order pick up and customer service.
  5. Assist floor staff with folding and putting new merchandise on the shelves.

Sales Floor

  1. Greet and assist customers with product location.
  2. Stock, fold and fill merchandise daily.
  3. Create displays and give feedback about merchandise.
  4. Perform other duties and assist in other areas of the Bookstore as needed and/or assigned.

Receiving

  1. Counting merchandise and accurately tagging items for the sales floor.
  2. Coordinating with sales staff shipment drop offs.
  3. Preparing shipments for on campus delivery.
  4. Assisting warehouse staff with organizing merchandise.


Qualifications:

  • Previous Sales Floor experience preferred. Some stockroom, warehouse, accounting, retail, library or bookstore experience helpful.
  • Outgoing and creative personality essential. Must be able to demonstrate professional and organizational skills, attention to detail, and an overall high level of reliability and independence.
  • Must Pay UCSD student services fees each quarter working.


Core Competencies:

  • Critical Thinking and Problem Solving
  • Oral Written and Digital Communication
  • Teamwork and Cross Cultural Collaboration
