Audrey’s Café is located on the second floor, East wing of UCSD’s Geisel Library. Named after Audrey Geisel, Dr. Seuss’s late widow, Audrey’s café blooms with creativity shining from our talented baristas. We’re stocked well with an assortment of sandwiches, snacks, and pastries, as well as certain medications like Emergen-C and Advil. Student-staffed, community-focused, and positivity powered, you can walk on over from your study table and equip yourself with a quick latte and pair it with one of our locally made Seb’s Paninis, ready to conquer your next exam.
