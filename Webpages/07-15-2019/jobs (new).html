JOB 1: Student 3/Loss Prevention Associate
 
Job Number: 2867755
Job Title: Student 3/Loss Prevention Associate
Wage/Salary: $13.00/hr
Work Schedule: Up to 19hrs/week (up to 30 hrs/wk during summer)
Location: UCSD Bookstore, La Jolla, CA
Number of Openings: 2
Start Date: ASAP
Must be able to work summer and a minimum 8 – 12 month commitment is requested.

Job Description:

General Loss Prevention Duties would include but not be exclusive to the following:
General Observation & surveillance in all parts of the Bookstore. Utilize camera system to watch for shoplifting & other suspicious activities. Deter and prevent wrongful taking of property. Detain suspected shoplifters. Assist UCSD Police Department with detentions & arrests. Prepare & Maintain reports, maintain daily log book. Appear in court as needed. Assist in emergency situations as needed. Other related duties as assigned.


Qualifications:

Retail experience required. Ability to maintain confidentiality, able to observe, identify, and report situations clearly. Computer literate in PCs.

 
Must pay UCSD student services fees each quarter working. Work-study preferred but not required.

**Background check required**
