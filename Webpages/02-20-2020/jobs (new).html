Job Number: 3538683

Number of Openings: 1
Start Date: ASAP


Qualifications:

Must be able to work independently and collaborate with team members while maintaining an accurate attention to detail. Ability to deal with a large volume of data, reset priorities based on the needs of the department and use active problem solving skills to investigate discrepancies. Prior data entry experience or 10-key preferred to ensure accuracy. Transferrable skills found in those who have some expertise with accounting, proof reading, analyzing invoices, retail sales, inventory management or bookkeeping. Work schedule: up to 19 hrs/week during the academic year and up to 29hrs/ week in summer. Shifts are Monday through Friday between 8am-4 pm.
Must pay UCSD student services fees each quarter working.



Core Competencies

  • Critical Thinking and Problem Solving
  • Professionalism and Integrity
  • Digital Information Fluency


Duties & Responsibilities

Assist the Bookstore Accounting staff with high volume data entry of invoices and assist with the weekly check run. Stock Control duties include data entry of packing slip information and matching paperwork, filing, and alphabetizing, matching, mailing, and other projects as assigned.

JOB 2: CUSTOMER SERVICE TEMP ASSOCIATE/STDT2

 
Job Number: 3570190
Job Title: CUSTOMER SERVICE TEMP ASSOCIATE/STDT2
Wage/Salary: $13.00/hr
Work Schedule: Up to 19 hours/week
Location: UCSD Bookstore, La Jolla, CA
Number of Openings: 10
Start Date: ASAP


Position Overview:

This is a temporary position running from March 9th through April 11th. Looking specially for students who can work some hours over spring break March 21-March 30th. A limited number of permanent positions may be offered with an increase from $13.00 to $13.25 at the end of the temporary appointment based on job performance, schedule, and needs of the bookstore.



Special Conditions of Employment:

The Sales Associate shifts are between the hours of 7:45am-6:15pm Monday-Friday and 10:45am-4:15pm on Saturday. During finals week the Bookstore opens 30 minutes earlier. Must be able to work some Saturdays. You will be scheduled between 12-18 hours per week (the opportunity to work more hours when school is not in session will be offered). If you have another on campus job you cannot work more than 29 hours total between each job. Please consider this if you have multiple jobs on campus as all hours worked will count toward this total.



Job Description:

Sales Floor
  1. Greet and assist customers with product location
  2. Stock, fold and fill merchandise daily
  3. Create displays and give feedback about merchandise
  4. Perform other duties and assist in other areas of the Bookstore as needed and/or assigned
Cashiering
  1. Ringing customers out at the register
  2. Greet customers and help them find product
  3. Help customers pick up online orders
  4. Direct customers to open registers, order pick up and customer service
  5. Assist floor staff with folding and putting new merchandise on the shelves
Receiving
  1. Counting merchandise and accurately tagging items for the sales floor
  2. Coordinating with sales staff shipment drop offs
  3. Preparing shipments for on campus delivery
  4. Assisting warehouse staff with organizing merchandise


Qualifications:

  • Previous Sales Floor experience preferred. Some stockroom, warehouse, accounting, retail, library or bookstore experience helpful
  • Outgoing and creative personality essential. Must be able to demonstrate professional and organizational skills, attention to detail, and an overall high level of reliability and independence
  • Must Pay UCSD student services fees each quarter working


Core Competencies

  • Critical Thinking and Problem Solving
  • Oral, Written, and Digital Communication
  • Teamwork and Cross Cultural Collaboration


Desired Start Date: 03/09/2020
