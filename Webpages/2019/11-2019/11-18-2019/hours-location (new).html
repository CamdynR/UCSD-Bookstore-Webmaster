 General Driving Directions to Gilman Parking Structure:
 
  •  
  • From North:
  •  
  • Take I-5 south.
  • Take the La Jolla Village Drive exit.
  • Turn right onto La Jolla Village Dr.
  • Turn right onto Villa La Jolla Dr.
  • At the end of Villa La Jolla Dr. enter Gilman Parking Structure.
  •  
  • From East:
  •  
  • Take I-8 west.
  • Merge onto I-5 north.
  • Take the La Jolla Village Drive exit.
  • Turn left onto La Jolla Village Drive.
  • Turn right onto Villa La Jolla Dr.
  • At the end of Villa La Jolla Dr. enter Gilman Parking Structure.
  •  
  • From South:
  •  
  • Take I-5 north.
  • Take the La Jolla Village Drive exit.
  • Turn left onto La Jolla Village Drive.
  • Turn right onto Villa La Jolla Dr.
  • At the end of Villa La Jolla Dr. enter Gilman Parking Structure.

Parking and Transportation For Visitors
Find options to park on UC San Diego campus.

 

Amazon@UCSanDiego
 
 Directions
  •  
  • Amazon@UCSanDiego is located on the first floor, right inside the UC San Diego Bookstore. If you enter 'UCSD Bookstore' in any maps app - it will lead you directly to the Amazon site.
  • To get here, enter through the Bookstore entrance on Lyman Lane. Or go downstairs by the fountain off Library Walk into Price Center Plaza. Or go up the stairs to the Price Center West entrance, through the building, left at door, and then down the stairs.
  • During the week, there is paid parking available, BUT parking is FREE on Sat and Sun anywhere on campus. The closest parking structure is Gilman Parking Structure. See directions above.
  • There is a 20 min loading zone parking available on Russel Lane, off the intersection of Myers Dr and Gilman Dr
