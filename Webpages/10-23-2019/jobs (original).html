  • Cashiering: Ringing customers out at the register.
  • Sales Floor: Putting merchandise out on the floor and helping customers. Processing sales orders. Greeting customers and showing them to product.
  • Receiving: Counting merchandise and accurately tagging items for the sales floor. Coordinating with sales staff shipment drop offs. Preparing shipments for on campus delivery. Assisting warehouse staff with organizing merchandise.

Core Competencies:

  • Critical Thinking and Problem Solving
  • Oral Written and Digital Communication
  • Teamwork and Cross Cultural Collaboration
