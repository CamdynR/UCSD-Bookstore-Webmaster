Job Number: 2822006
Job Title: CUSTOMER SERVICE ASSISTANT/STDT3
Wage/Salary: $13.00/hr
Work Schedule: Up to 19hrs/week
Location: UCSD Bookstore, La Jolla, CA
Number of Openings: 1

Qualifications:

Must be able to work summer. Retail experience essential. Energetic and upbeat person to provide customer service to internal and external customers of the UCSD Bookstore. Serve as backup cashier for sales floor during peak times and events. Help organize the stock room to improve the guest experience and sales. Enter student availability and student schedules into online software program for the scheduler. Assist the scheduler in contacting students concerning their availability. Organize documents for filing, scanning, photocopying and faxing. Capacity to process sensitive data while maintaining confidentiality. Check documents for accuracy and sort documents during off times to maintain accurate record keeping and avoid clutter. Promote Bookstore spirit and positive attitude and outlook to guests and students. Must be able to lift up to 50lbs with assistance and carry 25lbs.

Job Number: 2773631

Wage/Salary: $13.00/hr
