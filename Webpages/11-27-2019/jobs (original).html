JOB 1: Sunshine Market Cashier STDT2
 
Job Number: 3215301
Job Title: Sunshine Market Cashier STDT2
Wage/Salary: $12.25/hr
Work Schedule: Up to 19 hours/week
Location: UCSD Bookstore, La Jolla, CA
Number of Openings: 10
Start Date: 11/18/2019

Job Description:

Under supervision, the Sunshine Market cashier will perform clerical and manual related duties such as operating a cash register, cleaning out equipment, stocking and operating the coffee brewer. Primary responsibility of a cashier is serving the customer and processing their purchases:

  • Provide excellent customer service by greeting customers and answering their inquiries
  • Handle cash and card transactions using the cash register and credit card machine
  • Keep a clean and organized checkout area and sales floor
  • Operate the coffee and prepared foods (hot dog/soup) stations, including cleaning
  • Maintain aisles by stocking product and organizing shelves
  • Ensure proper operation of refrigerators and freezers by recording temperatures

Work Schedule:

Varied -

  • Up to 19 hrs/week
  • Monday-Thursday between 7:00am and 9:15pm
  • Friday 7:00am and 6:15pm
  • Saturday 11:00 am and 5:15pm
  • Sunday 11:00 am and 5:15pm

Core Competencies:

  • Teamwork and Cross Cultural Collaboration
  • Professionalism and Integrity
  • Oral, Written, and Digital communication

Qualifications:

Some retail or grocery store experience helpful. Work-Study preferred but not required. Background check required. Must pay UCSD service fess each quarter working. Work-study preferred but not required. Background check required.
