The UCSD Bookstore Sunshine Market, originally the Sunshine Store, opened in the 1970’s in what is now the UC San Diego Amazon center inside the Bookstore. It is now located in Price Center East. Open seven days a week ( closed Sundays in summer ) at the center of campus, its many features include:
 

  • 9 varieties of fresh brewed fair trade coffee
  • State of the art Pepsi Fountain dispensing 32 oz. servings for just .99 with flavor shots available at no extra charge!
  • A 24 foot five shelf cooler with fresh sandwiches and salads from 4 local vendors
  • Fresh baked pastries delivered Monday thru Friday from several local bakeries
  • Fresh sushi delivered Monday thru Friday
  • 18 doors of ice cold bottled and canned beverages from milk to soda to juice to coffee drinks, energy drinks, Yerba Mate, iced teas, and more!
  • 6 doors of frozen food featuring grab ‘n’ go / heat and eat sandwiches, burritos & entrees, and ice cream
  • The largest selection of snack foods on campus –  candy, salty, sweet, mixed, and hi protein meat snacks
  • The largest selection of grab ‘n’ go health and beauty aids on campus
  • Cleaning supplies, paper goods, basic groceries, and dorm necessities
  • Wide selection of dairy / deli items, including over a dozen convenient cheese, cheese / meat / cracker grab ‘n’ go snack packs
  • Organic, fair trade, non GMO, gluten free, vegetarian, vegan – yep! We’ve got a little of everything, and a lot of a lot of great things!
  • Stop by today and meet our awesome, friendly, helpful student staff and pick up a snack or a meal and a beverage!

Take advantage of our loyalty cards for hot drinks and soup, as well as fountain drinks. Buy 10 and the next one is on us!
