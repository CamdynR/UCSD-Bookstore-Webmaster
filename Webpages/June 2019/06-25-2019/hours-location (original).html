Parking and Transportation During Nights and Weekends
Find options to park on UC San Diego campus in the evening and weekends.

 

Amazon@UCSanDiego
 
 Directions
  •  
  • Amazon@UCSanDiego is located on the first floor, right under the UC San Diego Bookstore. If you enter 'UCSD Bookstore' in any maps app - it will lead you directly to the Amazon site.
  • Due to the construction on the first floor, access to Amazon is limited to the Price Center Plaza entrance only. To get here, you must either continue up Lyman Lane up to Library walk, turn right, then right at the fountain, down the stairs. Or go up the stairs to the Price Center West entrance, through the building, left at door, and then down the stairs. This is a temporary detour through early September.
  • During the week, there is paid parking available, BUT parking is FREE on Sat and Sun anywhere on campus. The closest parking structure is Gilman Parking Structure. See directions above.
  • There is a 20 min loading zone parking available on Russel Lane, off the intersection of Myers Dr and Gilman Dr
